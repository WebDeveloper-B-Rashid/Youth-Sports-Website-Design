About 6-Tool

Our mission is to empower young athletes to reach their full potential through technology and data-driven insights.

Empowering the Next Generation of Athletes

Founded in 2018, 6-Tool was created by a team of former athletes, coaches, and technologists who recognized the need for a comprehensive platform to support youth sports development.

Our platform combines cutting-edge technology with sports science to provide young athletes with the tools they need to excel in their chosen sports while developing valuable life skills.
